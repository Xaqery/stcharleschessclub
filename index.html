Schedule & Format

Every Thursday

  • 6:00–7:00 PM — casual / skittles games
  • 6:50 PM — sign up for ladder pairings
  • 7:00–10:00 PM — rated ladder games

Time control

80/d5 — eighty minutes per player with a 5-second delay.

Tournaments

Six club tournaments a year, including USCF-rated events. Top ladder finishers play for the Knight’s Cup and Squire’s Cup.

Join the Club

New players are always welcome. Your first two visits are free — just show up on a Thursday evening and ask for a game.

Annual membership is $39. Members appear on the weekly ladder and are eligible for club tournaments.
